---
title: 147. Snoopy Does Jogging
---
Snoopy jogs in the background from the right side of the screen to the left side. He turns around and jogs closer this time from the left side to the right. In the middle of the screen, he stops and jogs in place with a smile on his face. As in the previous animation, Snoopy wears a blue headband and a pink shirt. This animation can only occur during or immediately after a workout.
