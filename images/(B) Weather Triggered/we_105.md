---
title: 105. Snoopy Bursts out of a Snowman
---
A snowman is present on the screen at the beginning. Suddenly a crack appears in the snowman, and Snoopy bursts out of it! The snowman falls apart as Snoopy poses. He wears a green and red toque and a red scarf. This animation can only occur if it is snowing or it has recently snowed.
