---
title: 97. Snoopy Tries to Eat Ice Cream
---
Snoopy stands on the ground, holding an ice cream in the hot sun. He prepares to take a massive bite as he raises the cone to his mouth. However, as he takes a bite, the ice cream completely falls onto the ground. Snoopy bites his teeth instead. This animation can only occur when the daytime high temperature is greater than average.
