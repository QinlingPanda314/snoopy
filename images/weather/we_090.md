---
title: 90. Captain Snoopy is Blown by the Rain
---
Snoopy is dressed as a captain of a ship with his big sailor’s hat. He pretends to steer the ship on top of his doghouse. Heavy rain occurs and leaves fly by to show the wind. A very rare animation, it only occurs when there is freezing rain in a windy thunderstorm. Only a handful of people have ever seen it.
