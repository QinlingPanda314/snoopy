---
title: 103. Snoopy Skates and Spins on the Ice
---
Snoopy skates from the right of the screen to the left, then turns and spins from the left of the screen to the right. As he does this, he also does an exceptional spin. This is a reference to Snoopy the “Olympic Figure Skater” in Peanuts lore. Snoopy wears a long, green and red toque and figure skates. This animation is triggered by snow and the Christmas season.
