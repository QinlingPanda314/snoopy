---
title: 96. Woodstock’s Small Umbrella
---
Snoopy is sitting on the ground when it starts to rain. Woodstock comes in and sits on Snoopy’s head. Snoopy starts to smile when Woodstock starts to open an umbrella. He is annoyed when the umbrella turns out to be only just big enough for Woodstock himself. This animation only occurs when it is physically raining in your area.
