---
title: 39. Snoopy and Woodstock on the Swings
---
Snoopy is on a swing. One rope is connected to the center of the clock, and the other is connected to the minute hand. Woodstock tries to push Snoopy but eventually gets pushed out of the screen himself. The final reminiscence of Woodstock are 2 feathers on the ground. This animation can only occur when the minute hand is roughly pointed at 15.
