---
title: 137. Woodstock Drops Candy in Snoopy’s Bag
---
Snoopy walks into the screen, dressed as a bandit/hero/ninja (also seen in Animation 11.) He holds a bag for Trick-or-Treating. Woodstock flies into the screen holding a pink bonbon. He then drops it into Snoopy’s bag. They look happy. This animation can only occur on Halloween.
